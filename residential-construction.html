How we run a residential build

Fixed-price milestones by default. Standard schedule: 15% at contract, 25% at material delivery, 40% at rough-in complete, 20% at final walk-through. Change orders are written and priced before the trade lifts a tool — no surprise line items at close-out. One-year workmanship warranty on every trade we self-perform, transferable to the next owner if you sell inside 12 months. Weekly owner call with a two-week look-ahead so you always know what happens next and what to look at during your walk.
