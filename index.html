Who We Are

One Call.
Every Trade.

Big 7 Construction was built on a single idea: a client should never have to manage seven different contractors to build something great. We bring the full roster under one roof.

Our enterprise division handles large-scale commercial projects — multi-story buildings, warehouse complexes, industrial sites, and major tenant improvements. That's our flagship. But we didn't stop there.

Need a kitchen remodel? A leaking pipe fixed? A roof replaced before winter? We have licensed specialists for every trade in construction, held to the same standards we bring to a 100,000 sq ft commercial job.
