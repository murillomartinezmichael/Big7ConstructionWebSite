One Call.
Every Trade.

Big 7 Construction was built on a single idea: a client should never have to manage seven different contractors to build something great. We bring the full roster under one roof — commercial, industrial, and residential.

Our enterprise division handles the largest commercial and industrial projects — multi-story buildings, warehouse complexes, industrial sites, and major tenant improvements. That's our flagship. But we didn't stop there.

We also build custom homes from the ground up, complete full structural alterations, and service every residential repair your home needs — plumbing, electrical, roofing, HVAC. One call handles it all, held to the same exacting standard as a 100,000 sq ft commercial job.
