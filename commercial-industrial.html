The straight answers

Everything commercial clients actually ask about a bid.

Do you provide fixed-price bids?
Yes — fixed bids are the default. Every scope of work we quote comes with a hard number, a line-item breakdown, and a schedule. Time-and-materials is reserved for genuine unknowns. If your scope has T&M in it, we'll flag exactly which line items and why.
Do I own the drawings, permits, and closeout docs?
Yes. Final permits, warranties, closeout binders, and any project-specific drawings transfer to you at final payment. If we prepared design-assist drawings, you own those too. We keep archival copies for our records; you get the originals.
Can we scale the scope up or down mid-project?
Change orders happen; how we handle them is what matters. Every change gets a written change order with a fixed cost and a schedule impact, signed before work resumes. No surprises on the final invoice.
Do you take rush jobs — and what does that add?
Yes, when the calendar allows. Rush work runs 15–25% above standard bid, depending on trade availability and material lead time. You'll see the rush premium as its own line item — not folded into the base — so you know exactly what you're paying for.
